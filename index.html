The Phaser Protocol

Phaser is the ultimate passive income protocol generating yields to stakers of our PHX token through a network of innovative dApps. phaserstaking.com facilitates Phase I which is a year-long, yield-generating, endogenous ICO for an NFT robo-advisor.

About phaserstaking.IO

An original product making Defi more accessible.

phaserstaking is an ICO simulator which will fund the development of a yearn.finance style vault which proposes NFT strategies to help users interact with NFT platforms such as CryptoKitties optimally in order to maximize profits. Phase I will involve a contract presale offering discounted PHX tokens to investors, and the ICO simulator will immediately generate dividends for PHX-BNB LP token stakers. Phase II will be launched at the end of the ICO and will involve the deployment of the NFT vault.

FAQS

Frequently asked questions

  • What is phaserstaking.com?

    phaserstaking is Phase I of the wider Phaser Protocol, which hosts a year-long yield-generating endogenous ICO. Simply put, there will be a daily auction in which a decreasing amount of PHX tokens are sold. The BNB generated from those auctions will then be directly distrIbuted to stakers of PHX-BNB LPs.

    What does the public presale offer?

    The public presale offers a limit amount of tokens at a huge discount which grants early believers in the potential of our protocol the ability to realize considerable profits - Our presale represents a chance to get in early on something unique and great.

    Is the public presale safe?

    Yes. The public presale will be a programmable presale conducted through a smart contract coded in solidity using tried-and-tested best practices. Investors will receive PHX tokens immediately upon sending BNB to the presale contract. The contract will be verified on etherscan.

    What is the ultimate goal of the year-long ICO?

    The ultimate goal of the year-long ICO is to fund our NFT-robo advisor. Which we will begin building and improving upon the completion of our public presale.

    What is an NFT robo-advisor?

    Think of it as a yearn.finance style vault which proposes optimal NFT strategies instead of optimal yield-farming strategies. For example, for CryptoKitties our platform would guide users towards optimal breeding of cats in order to realize maximum profit in ETH upon the seling of those cats on NFT marketplaces such as OpenSea.
